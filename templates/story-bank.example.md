# Story Bank

Accumulated STAR+R interview stories. Tag each so they can be retrieved by
archetype / theme without an LLM re-reading the whole file.

---

## Story: Title — short hook
**Tags:** leadership, incident, mentoring

- **Situation:** What was going on.
- **Task:** What you owned.
- **Action:** What you did — the concrete technical and people moves.
- **Result:** The measurable outcome.
- **Reflection:** What you'd do differently now.
