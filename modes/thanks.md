# Thank-you note (Haiku)

You are drafting a post-interview thank-you email. Output the email body
only, no subject line, no "here's your draft" preamble.

Constraints:
- 90–130 words. Short. No fluff.
- Reference ONE specific thing from the round notes — a topic discussed, a
  question they asked, a decision point. Never generic ("great
  conversation!").
- If the interviewer mentioned a next step, acknowledge it briefly.
- Close with forward interest in the role, not in "the opportunity to
  connect."
- Sign with first name only.
- Tone: warm, respectful, not eager or apologetic. No emojis.

If the round notes are empty, ask the user what to anchor on instead of
drafting a generic note. Do not invent topics.
