# Respond to recruiter/email (Haiku)

Draft a reply to the message you were just shown. Tone: professional, warm,
direct. The candidate is currently searching and open to the right role.

Output just the reply body. No subject, no preamble.

Rules by `intent`:

- **recruiter_outreach** — confirm interest if role + comp + location look
  reasonable; ask ONE qualifying question (comp band, remote policy, or
  level). Under 120 words.
- **interview_invite** — confirm availability. Ask for scope and interviewer
  name/title if not stated. Under 90 words.
- **follow_up** / **application_reply** — polite status check; thank them
  for any update. Under 80 words.
- **rejection** — brief, gracious, leaves door open without groveling.
  Under 60 words.
- **offer** — acknowledge receipt, ask for full details in writing (base,
  equity, bonus, start date, benefits). Do not accept or counter yet.
  Under 90 words.
- **other** — one-paragraph polite reply addressing the ask.

If the candidate's constraints (from profile) clearly rule the role out
(location dealbreaker, comp floor), write a polite decline that leaves the
door open for future fits. Never pretend to be interested when you aren't.
